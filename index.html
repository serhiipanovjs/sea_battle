SEA BATTLE

Sea Battle Game Rules

Game Setup

Grid Size: The game is played on a 13x13 grid.

Ships: Each player has the following ships:

  • One Aircraft Carrier (length 5)
  • Two Battleships (length 4)
  • Three Cruisers (length 3)
  • Four Destroyers (length 2)
  • Five Submarines (length 1)

Ship Placement:

  • Ships can be placed either horizontally or vertically on the grid.
  • Ships cannot overlap or be placed diagonally.
  • Ships must be placed entirely within the grid boundaries.
  • There must be at least one cell space between ships (no touching even diagonally).

Gameplay

Shooting:

  • If the shot hits a ship, the system automatically announces "hit" and marks it on grids.
  • If the shot misses, the system announces "miss" and marks it on grids.
  • Hits and misses are recorded and displayed on the playerâ€™s tracking grid.

Sinking Ships:

  • When all cells of a ship are hit, the system announces the type of ship that was sunk.
  • The system keeps track of the remaining ships for each player.

Winning the Game:

  • The game continues until one player has sunk all of their opponent's ships.
  • The player who sinks all of their opponent's ships first wins the game.

Additional Rules

  • The system automatically verifies ship placements for legality before the game begins.
  • Player can re-position their ships during the setup phase until they confirm their final placement.
